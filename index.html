My Work

Master Thesis: How to Disappear Online

My thesis, titled "How to Disappear Online", explores the intricate concepts of digital erasure and personal privacy in the information age. It delves into the implications of the GDPR's "Right to be Forgotten", data sanitization practices, and the challenges of ensuring complete data erasure in a world of pervasive digital footprints. The thesis also examines various data recovery techniques, contrasting them with the need for effective digital erasure, and proposes new methodologies for ensuring personal privacy in the digital realm.

Focus Areas: Personal Privacy, Digital Erasure, GDPR Compliance, Data Recovery Techniques

Truthful vs. Deceptive Hotel Reviews

This project focuses on distinguishing between truthful and deceptive hotel reviews, showcasing my skills in handling complex classification tasks.

Technologies: Python

Helpful Review Predictor

Developed a Python script to predict the helpfulness of Amazon Fine Food Reviews. The project integrates machine learning models like Naive Bayes and Logistic Regression with a Hugging Face transformer for advanced text analysis. It predicts review helpfulness based on textual content, combining traditional ML models with modern NLP techniques and utilizes sentiment analysis for a nuanced understanding.

Technologies: Python, Scikit-learn, Hugging Face Transformers
